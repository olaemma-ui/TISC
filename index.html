Statement of Faith

We believe that the Bible is God’s unique revelation and that every word was supernaturally inspired by the Holy Spirit as it was being written down by various human authors. The Bible is our final authority for all matters of belief and behaviour (2 Tim 3:16). Events

Our Core Values

Values are what we consider important and worthwhile. Commonly held Core Values are the key to unity and are the foundation upon which all activity must be based. Paul encourages us to be “like-minded” and “one in spirit and purpose” (Phil.2:2). We are committed to developing and pursuing Biblical Kingdom values Events

Our Vision & Mission

Making disciples of all nations. "Matt 28:19"

This was the mandate of the Lord to the church as he ascended on high. As a church our strategy to obeying the Lord’s instruction is captioned in three words. We RECRUIT through evangelism and witnessing. We DISCIPLE through equipping meetings and we RELEASE men and women to their God given assignments what ever that may be in the light of spreading the course of the gospel of Jesus amongst the nations.
