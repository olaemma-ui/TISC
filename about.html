Our Leaders

Mr. & Mrs. Ajayi

We are privileged to serve the Lord has the lead Pastors for the Incorruptible seed church. Both stepped out in faith at the instruction of the Lord on April 6th, 2009, which happens to be the first service of the church in Lagos Nigeria. Keji had been an itinerant minister of the gospel. Holding crusades, preaching and starting fellowships in different locations and eventual starting a fellowship work on the 9th of April 1999 at Block 334 flat 4 in Jakande Estate Oke-Afa Isolo Lagos Nigeria. The fellowship was called The Estate Transformation network (ETN). Keji studied Urban and Regional planning at the University of Lagos while Jibike studied Electrical Engineering at the University of Ife in Nigeria. Both served faithfully in their then local church the Latter rain Assembly. Keji served has house fellowship Pastor while Jibike served in the ushering department. They were both commissioned into Ministry by Pastor Tunde Bakare. The serving overseer of the Citadel global community church formally called the Latter Rain Assembly. Keji and Jibike are blessed with three children. The church currently have centers in Africa and Europe. Keji and Jibike also serve has the conveners of Discipleship thrust and Endtime Missions.
